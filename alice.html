Meet Alice

Hi! I'm Alice. I am a lifelong learner and upper elementary and middle school teacher. I am excited to take on the challenge of becomming a web developer and empower kids to do the same. I empower kids to...

  1. Love learning, by:
    • Making learning fun
    • Meeting students where they are with differentiated instruction
    • Encouraging students to follow their interests

  2. Think critically about their environment, by:
    • Preparing students to get comfortable being stuck on a problem
    • Modeling collaboration with peers and mentors to solve problems
    • Inspiring students to question the status quo

  3. Harness their creativity with purpose, by:
    • Fostering artists, musicians, athletes, scientists, engineers...
    • Showcasing student work to an audience
    • Creating meaningful content that positively impacts the community and the world
